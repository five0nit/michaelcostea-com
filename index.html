Current Strategic Focus — AEH AI + Workflow Layer

AEH is building a central intelligence and workflow layer over its existing business systems. The layer is designed to unify knowledge, improve visibility across teams and systems, capture operational data more cleanly, embed AI into day-to-day workflows, consolidate reporting, and automate repetitive work safely.

  • Unify knowledge and improve visibility across teams and tools.
  • Capture operational data more cleanly at the source.
  • Automate follow-up, routine admin, and fragmented handoffs.
  • Speed up quoting, sales preparation, reporting, and exception surfacing.
  • Preserve human review where risk, quality, or customer trust matters.
  • Make dashboards operational control surfaces, not vanity reporting.

Operating Principles

  • Core systems remain in place; overlapping tools are rationalised over time.
  • APIs first, browser automation second, desktop/manual work as last resort.
  • Automation should be fail-closed, reviewable, measurable, and auditable.
  • Humans stay responsible for approvals, exceptions, trust, and edge cases.
