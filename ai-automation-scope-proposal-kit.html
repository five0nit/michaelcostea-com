Conservative by construction

Discount the theoretical saving before you show it.

The value model separates manual workload from the share that is actually recoverable, then applies a confidence factor. If monthly value does not clear monthly cost, the proposal says rescope—not “great ROI.”

  • Inputs stay visibleVolume, time, labour rate, recoverable share, confidence, setup cost, and recurring cost.
