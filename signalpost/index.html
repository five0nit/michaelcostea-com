Trust and control

Your account. Your media. Your final decision.

Minimum access

SignalPost requests basic creator information and Direct Post permission only. Provider credentials stay on the server.

No content harvesting

SignalPost accepts media the connected creator selects. It does not scrape, download, or republish arbitrary third-party content.

Delete the connection

Disconnecting removes the stored TikTok credentials and SignalPost session. Support handles additional deletion requests.
