# Discovery brief — Northstar Dental — fictional demo / New patient enquiry routing

**Prepared by:** Example Automation Studio  
**Date:** 2026-07-19

## Decision objective
Decide whether the written workflow is valuable, feasible, permissioned, and testable before committing to build.

## Current problem
Reception staff manually copy website enquiries into the CRM, check treatment type, assign an owner, and send an acknowledgement. Enquiries arriving after hours wait until the next shift and duplicate submissions are sometimes entered twice.

## Trigger
A new patient enquiry is submitted through the approved website form.

## Proposed happy path
1. Validate required consent and contact fields
2. Check for an existing CRM record using email and phone
3. Create or update one CRM record
4. Route by treatment type and location
5. Pause any external acknowledgement for staff approval during the pilot
6. Write a timestamped result or error receipt

## Systems involved
- Website form
- CRM
- staff approval inbox

## Measurable acceptance result
Ten approved test enquiries create or update exactly one CRM record, reach the correct owner, and produce a result receipt within two minutes.

## Evidence still required
- Client process owner confirms the current and proposed workflow.
- Frequency, effort, loaded cost, exception rate, and source-data quality are confirmed.
- Platform/API capability, permissions, privacy, and third-party fees are checked.
- Test records and named approvers are available.

## Known risk or dependency
CRM API access and field quality must be confirmed. Patient information requires client-led privacy review and minimum-access controls.

## Discovery decision
Proceed only if the client confirms the facts and the workflow can pass the acceptance plan.
