# Freelancer client admin — 10-minute setup checklist

This public preview shows the setup sequence included with Freelancer Client Admin Starter Kit.

1. Open the tracker and read `START HERE`.
2. Delete every clearly marked fictional example.
3. Add one consented lead.
4. Record one next action and follow-up date.
5. Before quoting, confirm deliverables, exclusions, revisions, timing, fee, currency, and payment terms in writing.
6. Track the next project milestone and due date; keep the signed agreement authoritative.
7. Before sending an invoice, check local invoice, tax, privacy, business-identifier, and record-keeping requirements.
8. Reconcile payment status against your bank or accounting source.

## Boundary

Operational checklist only. Not accounting, tax, employment, debt-collection, or legal advice. No guarantee of clients, payment, compliance, revenue, or business outcome.
