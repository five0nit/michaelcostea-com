The AP bottleneck

Your invoice data is already there. Your team still has to trust it.

Manual entry is only part of the problem. Finance teams need a reliable review layer that makes the obvious fields fast and the risky fields visible.

Manual entry tax

ABNs, invoice numbers, PO references, GST, due dates, subtotals, and totals get re-keyed across systems.

Messy intake

Invoices arrive through shared mailboxes, uploads, forwarded PDFs, folders, and ad-hoc supplier emails.

Exception blindness

A bad PO, missing GST split, duplicate invoice, or wrong supplier can slip through if the workflow only extracts text.

How the pilot works

A controlled AP workflow, not a generic OCR demo.

Start with a real batch, agree the fields, review exceptions, then export in the format the customer can actually use.

1Collect invoices

Email, upload, shared folder, or a sample batch from the customer’s current process.

2Extract fields

Vendor, ABN, invoice number, PO, dates, GST, subtotal, total, and document type.

3Flag exceptions

Missing PO, suspicious GST, duplicate-looking invoices, blank ABNs, and low-confidence fields.

4Export clean data

Excel, CSV, SharePoint, JSON, Power Automate handoff, or API-ready records.
